FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Which course is best for getting a job quickly?+
Tally ERP9 and ADCA are the best courses for quick employment. Tally is in very high demand in accounting firms, while ADCA gives you complete computer knowledge that most office jobs require. We recommend Tally if you are from a commerce background.
What are the batch timings available?+
We offer three batch timings: Morning (8AM–11AM), Afternoon (11AM–2PM), and Evening (4PM–7PM). You can choose the batch that fits your schedule when registering. We also offer flexibility to switch batches if needed.
Is the certificate government recognized?+
Yes, our certificates are recognized and accepted by employers. Upon successful completion of the course and exam, you receive a certificate that is widely valued in the job market for data entry, computer operator, and office assistant positions.
What is the fee structure for courses?+
We keep our fees very affordable compared to other institutes. The exact fees vary by course. Please call us at 9304633916 or visit our institute for the current fee structure. We also offer installment payment options for longer courses.
Can a complete beginner with no computer knowledge join?+
Absolutely! All our courses are designed for beginners. Manoj Sir starts from the very basics and ensures every student understands before moving forward. Many of our best students had zero computer knowledge when they joined.
Do you provide placement assistance after course completion?+
Yes, we provide placement guidance including resume building tips, interview preparation, and job search assistance. We have connections with local businesses and refer our top students for job openings that come to our notice.
