Our Story

The Journey Since 2010

📅 2010 — The Beginning

Maa Shakuntala Computer Classes Founded

Manoj Kumar Sharma started the institute with just a few computers and a big dream — to make computer education affordable for everyone in Danapur. Starting with DCA and basic MS Office courses, the institute quickly gained trust among students and parents.

📅 2014 — Expansion

Added Advanced Courses — Tally & Hardware

Responding to student demand and job market needs, Tally ERP9 and Computer Hardware courses were added. These quickly became the most popular courses as they directly led to job placements in local businesses.

📅 2018 — 200+ Students Milestone

Moved to Bigger Location & Multimedia Added

With growing demand, the institute moved to the current location at Uday Palace, Master Bazar. Multimedia, DTP, and Typing courses were introduced. The institute hit 200+ successfully trained students milestone.

📅 2025 — Present

500+ Students, 7+ Courses, Growing Every Year

Today, Maa Shakuntala Computer Classes stands as one of the most trusted computer institutes in Danapur with 500+ trained students, 7+ courses, and a proven track record of placement success. The journey continues!
